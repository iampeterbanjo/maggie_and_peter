Story

The first date that we went on was in Oxford, UK. We ate Japanese food for lunch and freshly made cookies for dessert as our hearts beat in tune with our footsteps on the cobbled stone. That evening we waited in the warmth of Starbucks for the last bus because neither of us wanted that day to end. Soon we will get our wish.
